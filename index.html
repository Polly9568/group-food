Victoria Sponge Cake



Induldge in yourself withthis traditional British dessert. The Victoria sponge cake! Topped with fresh strawberries and delicious cream, this is the perfect cake for any occasion. Enjoy this mouthwatering treat with your loved ones for afternoon tea.

Ingredients

  • 160g of unsalted butter
  • 160g of self-raising flour
  • 160g of caster sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 100ml of double cream
  • 125g of strawberry jam
  • 1 tbsp of icing sugar
  • 150g of sliced strawberries
  • 175g of icing sugar

Recipe Instructions

1. Pre-heat the oven to 170°C (150°C with a fan). Grease and line 2 x 8 inch cake tins with nonstick baking paper.

2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, vanilla extract and sugar using an whisk until light and fluffy.

3. Add the lightly beaten egg a tablespoon at a time, beating well between additions, until fully incorporated.

4. Carefully fold the flour in using a large metal spoon and spoon the batter evenly into the two prepared cake tins.

5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until springy to the touch and a cake tester comes out clean when inserted into the centre of the cakes.

6. Remove and allow the tins to cool on a wire rack for 5 minutes before turning out and peeling away the nonstick baking paper.

7.Whip the cream to soft peaks as the cakes cool, then spread the bottom half of the cake with the cream in an even layer.

8. Spread the strawberry jam evenly and carefully on top of the cream. Sandwich the cake with the other half of the cake and transfer it carefully to a serving plate.

9. Spread the remaining cream on the top of the cake and sprinkle with the sliced strawberries.

10. Dust with the icing sugar and enjoy!

Vegan Victoria Sponge Cake

Induldge in yourself with this traditional British dessert. The Victoria sponge cake! Topped with fresh strawberries and filled with a generous layer of vegan vanilla buttercream, this is the perfect cake for any occasion. Enjoy this mouthwatering treat with your loved ones for afternoon tea.

ingredients

  • 400g of self-raising flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp of bicarbonate of soda
  • 250g of caster sugar
  • 115ml of sunflower oil
  • 400ml of almond milk
  • 3 tbsp of golden syrup
  • 3 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 4 tbsp of strawberry jam
  • 150g of sliced strawberries
  • 200g of dairy-free spread
  • 175g of icing sugar

Recipe Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to gas 4, 180°C, fan 160°C. Grease and flour the sides and line the base of 23cm loose-bottomed cake tins.

2.Sift the flour, bicarbonate of soda and sugar into a large mixing bowl and mix together. Whisk together the sunflower oil, milk, syrup and vanilla in a jug and pour the mixture into the dry ingredients, then whisk using a hand-blender for 2 mins until thick and creamy.

3.Spoon the mixture into the prepared cake tins and bake for 35–45 mins until risen and cooked through. Remove from the oven and leave to cool for 15 mins in the tin, then remove and leave to cool completely on a wire rack.

4.While the cakes are cooling, make the filling: beat together the spread, icing sugar and vanilla in a mixing bowl, cover and leave to firm up in the fridge until needed.

5.Spread the jam evenly over one half of the cake, followed by two-thirds of the cream mixture. Top with the second cake, spread the remaining cream on top and decorate with the strawberries
